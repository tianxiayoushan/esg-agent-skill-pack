# IFRS S2 Source Map

Official source: IFRS Foundation IFRS S2 and supporting materials page.

## Readiness Map

- IFRS S2 focuses on climate-related risks and opportunities.
- It uses governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics and targets content areas.
- Readiness assessment should distinguish physical risks, transition risks, climate-related opportunities, transition plans, scenario analysis, and GHG metrics.
- Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 treatment requires methodology, boundary, data quality, category coverage, and assurance status review.

## Compliance Caution

Use readiness-only language unless every relevant requirement, reporting boundary, materiality judgment, metric, and disclosure location has been assessed and professionally confirmed.
