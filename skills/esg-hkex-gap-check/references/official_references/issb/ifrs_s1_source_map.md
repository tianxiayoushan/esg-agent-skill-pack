# IFRS S1 Source Map

Official source: IFRS Foundation IFRS S1 official page.

## Readiness Map

- IFRS S1 provides general requirements for sustainability-related financial disclosures.
- It focuses on information useful to primary users of general purpose financial reports.
- It uses content areas: governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics and targets.
- IFRS S1 and IFRS S2 are designed to be applied together when climate-related disclosures are prepared under ISSB Standards.

## Compliance Caution

Use readiness, alignment, or gap language unless the user provides a complete assessment, reporting basis, evidence, and professional confirmation.

Do not write `IFRS S1 compliant` or `ISSB compliant` by default.
