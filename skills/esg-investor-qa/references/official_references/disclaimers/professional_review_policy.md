# Professional Review Policy

Official references improve traceability but do not determine legal, regulatory, audit, assurance, financial, sustainability reporting, filing, or compliance outcomes.

Before external use, outputs require review by the relevant owners, which may include ESG, IR, company secretary, board office, securities affairs, legal, finance, EHS, operations, procurement, assurance providers, management, and the board.

Do not write final compliance conclusions unless the user provides official source text, company applicability facts, reporting period, evidence, and professional confirmation.
