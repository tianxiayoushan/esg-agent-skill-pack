# HKEX Appendix C2 Source Map

Official source: HKEX Rulebook Appendix C2 / ESG Reporting Code.

## Structure Map

- Part A: introduction and reporting principles.
- Part B: mandatory disclosure requirements and related reporting context.
- Part C: environmental and social KPI areas, generally requiring topic-specific applicability and materiality review.
- Part D: climate-related disclosures aligned around governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics and targets.

## Readiness Use

- Map user-provided disclosures to the relevant Appendix C2 area.
- Record source evidence, reporting period, issuer category, and obligation-level assumption.
- Do not convert a gap into “non-compliance” unless issuer status, rule text, reporting period, and legal/company-secretarial confirmation are supplied.
