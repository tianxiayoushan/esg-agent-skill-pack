# Copyright And Source Use Policy

- Do not copy full official rule text, full standards, full guidance documents, or long excerpts into this repository or generated outputs.
- Use short summaries, source-map labels, and official URLs.
- IFRS official materials are official sources, but IFRS Standards text is not reproduced here.
- HKEX, SSE, and SZSE official materials are used as source pointers, not republished rulebooks.
- Any commercial productization, redistribution, or automated extraction pipeline should undergo copyright and licence review.
